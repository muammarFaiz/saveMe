The Best Movies According to Faiz

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Lord of The Rings trilogy

okay, immediately the first movie that crossed my mind would be The Lord of The Rings trilogy. there is nothing like it that is close to its quality and massiveness.

Interstellar

for a movie about the space it definitely capture its vastness and some of its beauty and wonder. no movie ever done that to the level of Interstellar.

Avengers: Infinity War

this movie have the highest anticipation and hype that i ever remember. years of building up the hype, massive budget, and access to the most advanced tech in modern movie making. i think this movie have everyhting needed to be the highest grossing movie of its time.
